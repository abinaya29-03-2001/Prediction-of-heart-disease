Prevention

You can take several steps to reduce your risk for heart disease:

  • Don’t smoke. CDC’s Office on Smoking and Health Web site has information on quitting smoking. Learn more
  • Maintain a healthy weight. CDC’s Healthy Weight Web site includes information and tools to help you lose weight. Learn more
  • Eat a healthy diet. Tips on reducing saturated fat in your diet are available on the Web site for CDC’s Division for Nutrition, Physical Activity, and Obesity. Learn more
  • Exercise regularly. Visit CDC’s Physical Activity Web site for more information on being active. Learn more
  • Prevent or treat your other health conditions, especially high blood pressure, high cholesterol, and diabetes.
